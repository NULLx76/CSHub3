4.1 Bandwidth

There are a few contributions to the dynamic behavior of a network:

  • Feedback network (green):
  • Sets the intended dynamic behavior of the amplifier
  • We won't change this
  • Loop gain, controller (blue):
  • Finite static loop gain combined with poles and zeros of the loop gain. Controller was nullor, becomes practical operational amplifier.
  • We need to select the correct op-amp for the job
  • Parasitic impedances (red):
  • Impedances between terminals of controller and ground
  • We need to investigate its impact
