Polarization

Polarization is important in antenna problems: the received magnetic field should have the same direction as the receiver. If this isn't the case, you'll have 0 reception:

So: if you don't know how to properly polarize a wave, you'll get no reception.


Polarization: the direction of the E-field vector in a point as a function of time

The vector is directed along the propagation direction ﻿kkk﻿.
