7.6 Active and reactive power

The dissipated power (first term) is active power ﻿PPP﻿

The stored and returned power (second term) is reactive power ﻿QQQ﻿


The reactive power does have an effect on the rest of the system, since it needs to supply power in the supply cycle, and receive power in the other parts. This increases the current that the power supply needs to deliver and thus increases resistance.
